Introducing EcoDesign

Eco-friendly Goods

We are EcoDesign’s cofounders and a team of high school students, Hima Kolavennu and Mitali Naik. Our passion for environmental awareness and our interest in art led to the creation of our start-up company. We strive to promote environmental sustainability by selling art and interior design goods that are made using recycled and reusable materials. We believe that art can be found in the most unlikely places and can be made out of just about anything. The scrap paper you’re just about to throw out can be transformed into a wonderful showpiece. Our products not only represent the beauty of art but also the beauty of the environment. Our environment is what keeps us alive, and it is our responsibility to recognize and appreciate it. We plan to expand in the future and create a community that values environmental care.

What we do

At EcoDesign, we believe in sustainability and good vibes. Our products can give you both for a low cost. We work hard to make handmade products with love and care. We sell art, jewelry, trendy goods, stationery, organizational items, showcase pillows, and unique board games. Some of our products include mixed media paintings, framed art, bohemian earrings, friendship bracelets, protective face masks/shields, and mini foosball and pool. By purchasing our products, you are supporting global sustainability. Join our effort in giving back to our planet!
